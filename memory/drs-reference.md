## Company Facts

- DRS Engineering reference facts from the 2026 handbook: established in 2009 as a geo-structural engineering consultancy specializing in earth support systems and specialty foundations.
- Slogan: "SUPPORT YOU CAN RELY ON."
- Mission: design the most efficient, buildable, and safe earth retention and specialty foundations in the USA, with responsive service and employee growth.
- Core values emphasized in the handbook: technical competence, responsiveness, reliability, collaboration, respect, and innovation.
- Leadership structure from the handbook: Dave as President; Olivier for Design; Jody for Engineering; Luke for Operations.
- Luke also oversees Finance, HR, and Marketing; Jody leads Engineering and guides Neil; Olivier leads Design with Sydney supporting drafting/design.
- The firm is licensed in California, Washington, Oregon, Nevada, Utah, Colorado, Wyoming, and Montana.

## Statement Of Qualifications

- DRS Statement of Qualifications says the firm has completed over 500 projects since 2009.
- Services are positioned in three broad categories: Temporary Shoring, Permanent Earth Retention, and Specialty Foundations.
- Reference positioning: founded by Dr. David Salter after nearly a decade in Southern California earth-retention construction to provide competent, efficient, responsive earth-retention design without sacrificing quality.
- Clients range from homeowners to multinational developers.
- Support spans concept, detailed design, plans/specs, bid processing, and construction supervision.
