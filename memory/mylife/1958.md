# 1958

- Dave was born in 1958, probably on a Wednesday at about 4:00 in the morning.
- He was born at Bradford Royal Infirmary in Bradford, Yorkshire.
- He was a blue baby, meaning the umbilical cord was wrapped around his neck.
- Afterward, the family went home to 4 Green Cliffe Avenue in Baildon, which was the only home he knew then.
- He does not remember much directly from that year and knows it mostly from what was written in his baby book.
