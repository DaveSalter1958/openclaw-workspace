# 1968

- Dave thinks that after primary school, around 1968, he went to Bradford Grammar School, starting in the junior school called Thornville.
- Thornville was in a separate old house with grounds on Manningham Lane.
- His dad used to drop him off on the way to work; Dave really hated going in there.
- He remembers Thornville as a weird place with a funny headmaster who beat boys with a cane.
- Specific sensory memories: being hit on the knuckles with a cane, being hit on the backside with a cane, weird-smelling books, and walking to the main school for lunch every day along Manningham Lane and then through a gate/path into the school grounds.
- He thinks he was at Thornville for about two years and does not remember much else about it.
