# 1979

- Graduated from Nottingham University with a first-class honours degree in civil engineering.
- Went to Wales to work for French Kier Construction on Dinorwig Power Station.
- Worked as a young engineer, initially on night shifts, supervising miners drilling a huge shaft inside the mountain.
- The shaft was roughly 60 feet in diameter and about 2,000 feet deep; access involved wearing wetsuits and overalls and being lowered in a basket by winch.
- Helped lay out the shaft with laser beams so the drilling stayed vertical.
- Remembered the Welsh miners as tough and wild, working in horrible conditions and often playing tricks on young engineers, including filling suits with water and delaying the hoist.
- Lived in Bethesda for about a year.
- Shared or cared for a red setter named Bonnie with Ali.
- Lived in a cottage with another couple whose names Dave cannot remember.
- Dave and Ali, who were otherwise just good friends, slept together once.
- Broke up with Diane Howarth during this period, which was very sad.
- Climbed with Roger, including running up Idwal Slabs and other mountain routes.
- Drove a yellow Mini.
- Spent time around Gilgoth and slept in Roger's dorm.
- Remembered one night with a pretty Irish girl whose name he no longer recalls.
- Drank in a local pub that still used pre-decimal-style pounds, shillings, and pence calculations, which made buying beer funny and confusing.
- Remembered local characters, including someone growing a lot of weed in a basement, a landlord possibly named Richard, and a man known as Jack the Lad.
