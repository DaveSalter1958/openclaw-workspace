# 1981

- Continued PhD research at Nottingham University under Pete Davis, focused on traffic systems and automatic vehicle counting/classification.
- Worked from a small shed by the highway where traffic sensors were installed in the road and connected to Dave's computer systems.
- Recorded traffic data and wrote programs to identify the type of vehicle that produced each sensor pattern.
- Lived in his Nottingham house with the downstairs bathroom off the kitchen, a room containing his grandmother's piano, a sofa near the wall, and bedrooms upstairs.
- Played cricket and spent time with Nottingham friends and colleagues, including John Sheehan, John Hughes, Dave Hughes, Mike Benson, and others.
